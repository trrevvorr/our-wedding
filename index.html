About Us

We first met in 2015 during an interview with Epic in Madison Wisconsin. We talked a little but knew the likelihood of ever seeing each other again was slim to none. Nancy headed back to her Junior year at Michigan state and Trevor his Senior year at Missouri S & T.

However, we both got offers for the internship and became friends. We had a great summer working and hanging out with our intern friends, but eventually the summer ended and we headed back to school once again.

We kept in contact though and once we both got offers to come back to Epic full time, we started wondering if this was meant to be. We met up to go camping on September 11th of 2016... and the rest is history.
